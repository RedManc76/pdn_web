A life committed to holiness.

Humility, to be desired more than health, freedom, and possessions.

A desperation and crying out to God to grow in the fruits of the spirit.

A dedication to the word of God and a heart that longs for the mysteries contained in it.

A daily burden to grow more like Christ.

Deep conviction for sin and a desire to run from it.

A greater understanding of the cost of the cross.

A greater passion and ability to memorise scripture.

To know and apply scripture for every situation and decision I must make.

Faith that moves mountains.

A life totally surrendered to God and His purposes, no matter what.

Joy in suffering.

An intimacy with God that cost me everything and a willingness to pay it.

A greater intimacy with the Holy Spirit and a desire to walk in step with Him.

A passion to forsake those things of the world that limit the things of the Spirit.

A willingness to forsake all things for the glory of God.

The privilege of a martyr's crown, to be taken when my death brings greater glory to God than to live.

To truly say and live out with the Apostle Paul, 'for me to live is Christ, to die is gain'.

A desire that men and women of like passion be raised up and a sacrificial heart to encourage them.

A heart that is always in total tune with God and His will.

A life dedicated to prayer.

A faith that understands the promised answers to prayer and pursues them no matter what.

That my greatest desire and longing is to be in secret prayer with God.

A greater thirst for fellowship with God and prayer than for food and water.

A heart that will pay the greatest of costs to draw closer to God in secret prayer.

An inner thermostat that highlights my need to flee to a place of secret prayer.

To hear the Spirits prompting immediate prayer for those unseen dangers and trials.

An increased desire in the saints for all these things.

To pray without ceasing.

That the Holy Spirit leads me into a deeper and more powerful ministry in prayer.

God to raise up a people of prayer for my area.

Put vision and burden on their hearts.

God to raise up prayer leaders and coordinators.

Corporate unity in prayer.

Desperation to see God move in power.

Wrestling with God until a prayer burden has been granted.

Men and women that weep over the lost.

Local church to grow in number, love, unity, and fellowship.

Strategy for moving forward.

God to raise up leaders.

Fellowship groups, starting and growing.

Strategy and teams for reaching the lost.

Outpouring of God's Holy Spirit to save the lost and transform the area.

An outpouring like England has not seen before.

One that receives secular national recognition and shows all that our God is alive and reigns.

one that will flow out and affect the whole nation.

UK to be so set ablaze by God that burning embers will rise out of her and impact the nations.

God to be glorified in everything that's done.

The gospel message to go out in power.

The gravity of the gospel message to be felt.

The weight of conviction of God's holiness.

Conversions to be true.

Greater holiness in the church so that the separation between it and the world is wide and distinct.

The weight and gravity of the Christian pursuit of God to be lifelong and life-changing.

The majesty and heaviness of God's presence to be felt in the community.

People come running under conviction to hear the gospel message preached.

A greater understanding of the beauty of the gospel and a desire to see more of it.

A broken heart and bitter tears for the lost in town, nation, and world.

A heart that is burdened for those that are lost.

A prayer burden that cannot be ignored but must cry out to God.

A total reliance on the sufficiency of God to meet all of the church's needs.

Prompting of the Spirit during the day or night to pray for that which is on the heart of God.

Looking to God for His strength alone and be constantly aware of His sufficiency.

Faith to believe and grab hold of the promises of God no matter what happens in the natural.

An expectancy of great things and a faith-filled pursuit of them in prayer.

One or two prayer partners with the same heart for prayer.

A raising up of intercessors in their own area of influence so that our country is saturated in prayer.

To see once again in our country the power and influence of intercessory prayer.

That the next outpouring on our nation be the greatest ever seen and come soon for the glory of God.

A heart that suffers with those that are suffering.

An empathy that enters into others suffering.

Strategy for meeting the physical, emotional, and spiritual needs of the lost.

Raising up of men and women with the same heart and desires to meet their needs.

Strategy for building infrastructure.

Faith that looks to God alone to provide resources.

Boldness in proclaiming the gospel.

Men and women with totally surrendered lives will be raised up for the work of the kingdom.

People longing to be discipled and godly people to do it.

People desperately longing for more of God.
