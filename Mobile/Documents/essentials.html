Download Prayer List

Bring us into greater intimacy with your Holy Spirit; Grant us the mind of Christ and break our hearts for what breaks yours.

Help us to appreciate your precious intimate presence and guard it jealously with all of our being and strength.

Share Christ's intercession by his Spirit, concerning those things he wants to do in and through us.

Grant us faith so that we might trust your leading, know your hearing and expect your answers.

Cause our faith to be steadfast and sure, not faltering or ceasing before we have what we are asking for.

Lord, cause our faith to grow as we witness increasingly powerful answers to our prayers.

Make us holy and vessels fit for the Master's use by the sanctifying power of the Holy Spirit.

Give us a hatred of those things in our lives that hinder prayer and cause us to fall upon your mercy until they are eradicated.

Give us a greater zeal to see our brothers grow in faith and prayer and be mighty used for Christ's glory.

Help us to see the cross in all its glory.

Give us a heart that longs for God's glory, and everything we do be done for that chief end.

Conform our hearts, minds, and wills to your word and grant us a greater zeal to know it and a greater love to apply it.

Grant us a greater love for prayer and communion with the living God than anything else in our lives.

Increase our love for one another, so that we prefer one another and rejoice to see others used more than ourselves.

Protect our unity in prayer from the schemes of the evil one.

Each new member is prepared by God, joins the right group, and grows in his or her understanding of the seriousness of this work and their usefulness to it.

Prepare leaders to lead in accordance with our values, in great solemnity and to facilitate the work of the Spirit.

New gatherings to be planted and used mightily for the Lord's glory.

The gospel to go out not only in word but in power, transforming lives, pulling down strongholds, and glorifying Christ.

The Church in our nation turns from her idols of ease and comfort and pursues the glory of Christ with all vigour and zeal.

Revival in our nation: the kingdom of Christ advanced, and the kingdom of darkness crushed under the foot of the church with Christ seen victorious as its head.
